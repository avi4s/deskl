    FAQ

    What Else You Want to know

    + What is no code?
    Users are empowered with no-code technology to create, manipulate, and employ data-based applications to do their work better. No-code applications are a part of the self-service movement that removes the need for programming or knowledge of programming languages.
    + Will my application scale?
    We create apps that are designed to increase in size, based on our knowledge of what advantages and user behaviour the client wants. The technology is scalable and may be expanded as necessary to serve a growing user base. By monitoring app capacity utilisation, you may adjust it as necessary.
    + What platforms do you use to build apps?
    Almost all of our projects are built on low-code technologies,but we use third party integrations. From offline capabilities to payment integration to virtual and augmented reality, we use platforms like Glide,Alido and webflow.
    + How much will my application cost to build?
    We charge by the amount of work involved with our assignments. The amount of content you wish to include, how difficult it is to programme, and whether you prefer our design framework or create your own are the most significant factors. Please contact us if you require a quote for your project.!
    + Can you develop without coding?
    Yes and the No Code tools and methodologies we use have been in the market for several years and have been used in thousands of successful projects, which guarantees long-term sustainability.All the tools we use have an emergency plan in case they cease to exist.
    + Do your applications respond to users' input?
    Depending on the requirements of the project and how they will use it, we can design applications accessible via mobile phones, tablets, and computers.
    + What is the development time for a low code/no code project?
    We usually spend between 3 - 5 months on each project depending on its size. Although size and launch of the project plays an important role too in completing the project.
    + What projects have you done till now?
    We have worked with companies and entrepreneurs in health sectors, social- media, delivery, banking, fitness apps and many others.
